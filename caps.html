Welcome to CAPS!

CAPS provides free, confidential counseling services to support students' mental health and well-being. We offer both in-person and virtual appointments.

Located on the 4th floor of the Michigan Union, our Front Desk is open to help you schedule appointments and answer questions. Call us at734-764-8312.

Need a private space for telecounseling? Limited rooms are available - contact the Front Desk for details.

For medical services, visit theUHS website.

CAPS Support Programs

Let's Talk

Let's Talk is a free program that supports students starting their mental health journeys. It provides casual meetings with counselors during specific drop-in times for initial support and advice.Learn more

Stress Less

Stress Less is a drop-in back to school guide for navigating various challenges - whether it's managing time effectively, coping with uncertainty, or balancing academics and social life. It provides stress-management techniques, strategies for staying organized, mindfulness and relaxation exercises, and teaches coping skills for managing anxiety.Learn more

Individual Peer Counseling

Individual Peer Counseling (IPC) is a program designed to offer a new way to meet the needs of students across the University of Michigan. We are here to support student mental health and offer students a place to vent.Learn more
