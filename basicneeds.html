Welcome to Basic Needs!

We believe in fair and equitable access to food and other basic resources. Because when each of us has what we need, our community is stronger and our students are more successful.

Resources by Category

Critical Incident Support

Everyone deserves to be in an environment where they feel safe and respected. When a conflict or critical incident impacts your education and wellbeing, it shouldn’t go unresolved. Turn to these resources for help finding support:

  • Equity, Civil Rights, and Title IX
  • Student Legal Help
  • Dispute Resolution Center
  • Sexual Assault Prevention
  • Explore Additional Resources

Finance

Students may find it difficult to make ends meet, especially with the demands of classes, papers and exams. But you don’t have to do it alone. If you’re facing financial issues or concerns, these resources are available to assist:

  • Student Loans
  • Child Care Subsidy
  • Student Emergency Funds
  • Work Study
  • Explore Additional Resources

Food

Students should go to class hungry for knowledge, not for food. If you can’t afford balanced meals or sacrifice food to pay for bills, know that you don’t have to choose. Turn to these resources for help:

  • Food Finder
  • Maize & Blue Cupboard
  • Michigan Dining Nutrition Services
  • Student Food Co.
  • Explore Additional Resources

Housing & Transportation

Students have a lot to consider when heading off to college. Where will I live? How will I get to class? Is there a bus to the grocery store? Use these resources to find answers for all of your questions and needs:

  • Family Housing
  • Emergency Ride
  • HomeShare Program
  • TheRide
  • U-M After-Hours Transportation
  • Explore Additional Resources

Health Care

An important part of being away at college is remembering to take care of yourself. From scheduling an annual checkup to seeking emergency care, you have options for making your health a priority. Turn to these resources for help:

  • Hope Clinic
  • Packard Health
  • Medicine Assistance Tool (MAT)
  • The Corner Health Center
  • University Health Service
  • Explore Additional Resources
